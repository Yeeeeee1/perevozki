Road Transportation

SOLVEX SP.Z.O. provides cargo delivery (LTL,FTL) by road from China to Europe, from Europe to Azerbaijan, Uzbekistan, Kazakhstan, Georgia and Armenia using the door-to-door method, carrying out transportation even to places difficult to access for other types of transportation. The effectiveness of this service is confirmed by the rapid delivery of transport for loading, the absence of the need for intermediate cargo operations, which is especially important when transporting equipment from China.

The speed and flexibility of our colleagues will not leave unresolved the questions of our customers in the following types of transportation:

  • Combined cargo (LTL);
  • import/export of containers to/from ports in Europe, China, USA, Africa during export/import of goods;
  • Container delivery and removal to / from railway stations in Europe and China;
  • agricultural and construction machinery;
  • oversized cargo;
  • registration of CMR, TIR, T-1 transport documents;
  • export registration of documents in Europe EX-1;
  • Transshipment and consolidation of goods in warehouses in China, Poland, Lithuania, Germany;
  • Cargo insurance.

Our specialists will select the optimal method of cargo delivery, taking into account its features, characteristics and the wishes of the client. We will contact the shippers to clarify the availability dates and the best time to load the vehicle. For the convenience of our customers, we will take care of all issues that arise along the way of the car. Having direct and long-term relationships with international logistics and transportation companies allows us to offer a competitive and flexible pricing policy.
