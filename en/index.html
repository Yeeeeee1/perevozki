EXPLANATIONS

  1. A license to perform road transport in the scope of intermediation in the transport of goods does not entitle the holder to perform road transport.

  2. The entrepreneur is obliged to report in writing or in the form of an electronic document to the body that granted the license to perform road transport in the scope of intermediation in the transport of goods, any changes in the data referred to in Article 8 of the Act of 6 September 2001 on road transport, no later than within 28 days from the date of their occurrence. If the changes include the data contained in the license, the entrepreneur is obliged to submit an application to change the content of the license.

  3. The license to perform road transport in the scope of intermediation in the transport of goods covers intermediation in domestic and international transport.
